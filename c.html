Overview of SGPA and CGPA

Semester Grade Point Average(SGPA):

SGPA, or Semester Grade Point Average, is a weighted average that measures a student's academic performance in a specific,single semester. It is calculated using the following formula:

Formula:

SGPA=Sum(credit X Grade point)/Sum(Total credits)

Cumulative Grade Point Average(CGPA):

CGPA, or Cumulative Grade Point Average, is a metric used to measure your total academic performance across all Semesters or years of your course. While SGPA focuses on a single semester, CGPA provides the big picture of your entire journey.

Formula:

CGPA=Sum(All semseter SGPA)/Total number of semesters
